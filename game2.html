Exciting and Addictive Unity-Based Game

Floppy Ball is an exciting and addictive Unity-based game where players control a bouncing ball, navigating it through a series of obstacles. The ball automatically jumps, and the player must time the jumps precisely to avoid hitting walls and obstacles that appear on the screen. The goal is to score as many points as possible by successfully passing through gaps in the obstacles. With fast-paced gameplay, simple controls, and increasingly difficult levels, Floppy Ball keeps players engaged and challenged as they strive for higher scores.
