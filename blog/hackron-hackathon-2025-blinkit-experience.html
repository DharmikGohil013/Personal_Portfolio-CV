The Challenge: Waste Management Automation in Dark Stores

Blinkit presented us with a real-world problem that their dark stores face daily: Waste Management Automation. Dark stores are essentially mini-warehouses optimized for quick online deliveries, and efficient waste management is crucial for maintaining hygiene, reducing costs, and ensuring smooth operations.

Problem Statement
  • Challenge: Automate waste segregation and management in Blinkit's dark stores
  • Goal: Improve operational efficiency and reduce manual labor
  • Impact: Enhance sustainability and hygiene standards
  • Technology Focus: IoT, Automation, Data Analytics
  • Time Limit: 24 hours to ideate, develop, and present

Our Solution: SafaiNova

Our team developed SafaiNova - an intelligent waste management automation system specifically designed for dark store environments. SafaiNova combines IoT sensors, machine learning, and automated sorting mechanisms to revolutionize how waste is handled in quick-commerce warehouses.

SafaiNova Key Features
  • Automated Waste Classification: AI-powered categorization of waste types
  • Smart Bin System: IoT-enabled bins with fill-level monitoring
  • Real-time Analytics Dashboard: Track waste patterns and optimize collection
  • Predictive Maintenance: Alert system for bin capacity and collection schedules
  • Integration Ready: Seamlessly connects with existing dark store management systems
  • Sustainability Metrics: Track environmental impact and recycling rates

The 24-Hour Journey

The hackathon was structured as a continuous 24-hour challenge, testing not just our technical skills but also our stamina, teamwork, and creative problem-solving abilities.

Timeline Breakdown
  • Hour 0-2: Registration, team formation, and problem statement reveal
  • Hour 2-6: Brainstorming and solution ideation phase
  • Hour 6-12: Core development and prototyping
  • Hour 12-18: Feature implementation and integration
  • Hour 18-22: Testing, debugging, and refinement
  • Hour 22-24: Final touches and presentation preparation
  • Final Hour: Project presentation and judging
